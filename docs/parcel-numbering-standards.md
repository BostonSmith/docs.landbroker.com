## Parcel Numbering Standards

Parcel Number Standards ensure that internal and external users number parcels consistently.

### West Virginia

West Virginia parcels should follow the XX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX format. Below are some examples:

- 01-B36B-0016-0000
- 12-0001-0005-0002

### Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania parcels should follow the XXX-XXX-XX-XX-XXXX-XX format where possible. Below are some examples:

- 230-006-00-00-0015-00
- 230-014-00-00-0015-04
- 230-007-01-02-0001-01
